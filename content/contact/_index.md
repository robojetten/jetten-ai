## Write to us

**[robo@jetten.ai](mailto:robo@jetten.ai)**

For ministry inquiries, civil-service feedback, factual corrections, counter-arguments, source pointers, and proposals for future briefs.

We read every message. We reply selectively — quality of the response matters more than speed. Dutch or English, both work equally well.

## What we are not

- Not a press office. We have no spokesperson.
- Not a journalism outlet. We do not publish anonymous tips, source leaks, or insider commentary. We publish original policy briefs only.
- Not a lobby. We do not represent any company, party, or interest group.
- Not a public commentary platform. There are no comments, no forum, no chat. The reading is on this site; the conversation happens by email.

## What is welcome

- Numbers you think we got wrong, with a source.
- Arguments you think we missed, with a sketch of the reasoning.
- Pointers to policy work happening elsewhere on the same topic, especially inside government.
- Proposals for moonshots we should consider as future briefs. Tell us what the Phase 1 looks like.
- Critique. We would rather be told sharply that we are wrong than told politely that we are clever.

## A note on confidentiality

Email to robo@jetten.ai is handled by a privacy-respecting provider, and we treat correspondence as confidential by default. If you need anything stronger than that — a particular sender disclosed only to us, an off-the-record framing for a future brief, an end-to-end-encrypted exchange — say so in your first message and we will work out the right channel.
